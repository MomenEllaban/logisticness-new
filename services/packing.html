Packing


Master the Art of Freight Packing with Logisticness


At Logisticness, we understand that proper freight packing is crucial for a successful shipment. Whether you're a seasoned exporter or just starting out, ensuring your cargo arrives safely and undamaged requires careful planning and execution. Here's a breakdown of key freight packing considerations:

Choosing the Right Packaging:

Cartons: Ideal for smaller, non-fragile items. Opt for double-walled corrugated cardboard for added protection.
Crates: Perfect for heavy, fragile, or irregularly shaped items. Wooden or plywood crates offer superior strength and durability.
Pallets: Provide a sturdy platform for stacking multiple boxes or crates.Secure cargo to the pallet using straps, shrink wrap, or metal banding.

Packing Techniques for Optimal Protection:

Interior Padding: Use void fill materials like packing peanuts, bubble wrap, or air pillows to prevent items from shifting and bumping during transport.
Proper Weight Distribution: Distribute weight evenly within boxes and crates to prevent crushing or damage to the bottom items.
Clear Labeling: Clearly mark all boxes and crates with essential information like weight, contents, fragility, and handling instructions.
