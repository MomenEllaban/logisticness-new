Breakbulk


Conquer Complex Cargo with Logisticness' Breakbulk Solutions

Logisticness isn't just about the big stuff. We excel in handling breakbulk shipments, ensuring your unique and oversized cargo reaches its destination safely and efficiently.

Breakbulk shipments present logistical challenges. Logisticness steps in to provide a seamless experience, from planning to delivery.

Why Choose Logisticness for Your Breakbulk Shipment?

  • Expert Project Management: Our dedicated team coordinates every step of your breakbulk shipment, ensuring smooth execution and clear communication.
  • Customizable Solutions: We tailor our services to your specific cargo needs,considering factors like size, weight, fragility, and required handling procedures.
  • Global Network: We have established relationships with reliable carriers and port operators worldwide, guaranteeing access to the best routes and infrastructure.
  • Safe and Secure Transport: We prioritize the safety of your cargo, utilizing specialized equipment, secure storage, and experienced personnel.

Our Breakbulk Services Include:

  • Pre-shipment Planning: Our team meticulously plans and assesses your breakbulk shipment, considering factors like route options, port facilities, and required permits.
  • Packing and Crating: We offer expert packing and crating services to ensure your cargo is protected against potential damage during transport.
  • Loading and Unloading: Our skilled personnel utilize specialized equipment for safe and efficient loading and unloading at origin and destination ports.
  • Customs Clearance: Our team handles all customs clearance procedures,ensuring your shipment navigates through ports with minimal delays.
  • Inland Transportation: We can coordinate inland transportation from port terminals to your final destination.
