Dry Bulk


Our Dry Bulk Services Include:

  • Pre-shipment Planning: Our team will assist you in selecting the most appropriate vessel for your cargo type and volume, considering factors like loading/unloading facilities and route optimization.
  • Chartering and Negotiations: Our experienced chartering specialists will negotiate competitive freight rates on your behalf, ensuring you receive the best possible value for your dry bulk shipment.
  • Cargo Handling and Documentation: We'll manage all aspects of cargo handling, from loading and unloading to securing permits and preparing necessary documentation.
  • Customs Clearance: Our team will ensure your dry bulk shipment seamlessly clears customs at both origin and destination ports.
  • Post-shipment Support: We'll continue to monitor your shipment even after it reaches its destination, providing ongoing support and assistance.
