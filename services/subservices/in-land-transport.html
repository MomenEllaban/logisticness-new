Inland transport


Your journey doesn't end at the port: Seamless inland transportation with Logisticness

Sea freight is a vital piece of the puzzle, but getting your cargo to and from the port is just as important. That's where Logisticness comes in. We offer a comprehensive range of inland transportation options to ensure your shipment arrives smoothly at its final destination.

Intermodal expertise: The efficient way to move your cargo

Our inland transportation services are designed to seamlessly connect with your ocean freight. This is known as intermodal transportation, where your cargo stays in the same container throughout its journey. This reduces handling and simplifies the process, saving you time and money.

Logisticness: Your trusted partner for every leg of the journey

We don't just offer trucks, we offer peace of mind. Our team will ensure your cargo arrives safely and on time. Trucks are fully equipped with GPS tracking, so you can monitor your shipment every step of the way.
