Ship small, save big: Less Than Container Load (LCL) with Logisticness

Don't have enough stuff to fill a whole shipping container? No problem! Logisticness offers a smart solution for smaller shipments with our Less Than Container Load (LCL) service.

Perfect for your needs:

  • Cost-Effective: Why pay for a whole container when you only need a little space? LCL lets you share a container with other shippers, reducing your shipping costs significantly.
  • Ideal for Small Shipments: Got a small batch of goods to move? LCL is perfect for situations where a full container isn't necessary.
  • Fast and Flexible: Need your shipment delivered quickly? You don't have to wait to fill a whole container with LCL.
