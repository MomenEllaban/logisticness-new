RoRo Shipment


Streamline Your RoRo Shipments with Logisticness

At Logisticness, we understand the unique challenges of RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off) shipping. Whether you're transporting brand new cars, heavy machinery, or any other type of wheeled cargo, our team of experts has the knowledge and experience to ensure a smooth and efficient journey.

Benefits of Choosing Logisticness for Your RoRo Shipment:

  • Extensive Network: We have established partnerships with leading RoRo carriers,offering you access to a wide range of routes and schedules.
  • Competitive Rates: We leverage our strong relationships to secure the best possible rates for your RoRo shipment.
  • Expert Guidance: Our team will handle all aspects of your RoRo shipment, from customs clearance and documentation to route planning and insurance.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Enjoy peace of mind with our robust tracking system,allowing you to monitor your cargo's progress every step of the way.
  • Safe and Secure Transport: We prioritize the safety of your cargo, utilizing secure RoRo vessels and experienced personnel to ensure your shipment arrives damage-free.

Our RoRo Services Include:

  • Pre-shipment Planning: We'll work with you to determine the most efficient and cost-effective route for your RoRo shipment, considering factors like size, weight,and destination.
  • Customs Clearance: Our experienced team will handle all necessary customs documentation, ensuring a seamless transition through ports.
  • Port Operations: We'll coordinate with port authorities and terminal operators to ensure your cargo is loaded and unloaded efficiently and safely.
  • Insurance Options: We can provide you with a variety of insurance solutions to protect your RoRo shipment against potential risks.
